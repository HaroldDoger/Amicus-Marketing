Services

What we have to offer

At Amicus Marketing, we offer a full range of marketing services designed to help businesses succeed. Our main services include branding, advertising, public relations, and digital marketing.

Branding:

Our branding services help businesses establish a strong, recognizable brand identity. We work with clients to create a unique and memorable brand that stands out in their industry. Benefits of our branding services include increased brand recognition and customer loyalty, improved customer engagement, and a more professional image. Past projects include branding for a tech startup, a local restaurant, and a non-profit organization.

Advertising:

Our advertising services help businesses get the word out about their products and services. We create effective, eye-catching ads that grab attention and drive results. Benefits of our advertising services include increased visibility, improved brand recognition, and better engagement with target audiences. Past projects include a nationwide advertising campaign for a retail chain, a local restaurant's Grand Opening ads, and a product launch campaign for a tech company.

Public Relations:

Our public relations services help businesses build and maintain strong relationships with key stakeholders, including customers, media outlets, and influencers. We craft compelling stories and messaging to help clients achieve their PR goals. Benefits of our PR services include increased media coverage, improved brand reputation, and greater customer trust. Past projects include a media relations campaign for a tech startup, a crisis management campaign for a local business, and a brand launch event for a non-profit organization.

Digital Marketing:

Our digital marketing services help businesses reach and engage with customers online. We create and execute effective digital marketing campaigns that drive traffic, leads, and sales. Benefits of our digital marketing services include increased online visibility, improved customer engagement, and better conversion rates. Past projects include a social media marketing campaign for a local restaurant, a Google Ads campaign for a retail chain, and an email marketing campaign for a non-profit organization.
